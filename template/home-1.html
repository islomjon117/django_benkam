About the company

BENKAM was founded in 2015 with the aim of producing high-quality aluminum profiles for various industries, including construction, automotive and energy.

Our facility is equipped with six modern extrusion presses from Italian and Korean manufacturers, with capacities ranging from 1,000 to 4,000 tons. We operate two vertical powder coating lines that support profiles up to 7 meters in length, and one horizontal coating line for profiles up to 12 meters. The production site also includes an anodizing line and a decorative coating (sublimation) line that replicates wood texture and other decorative effects. The surface treatment section features satin finishing, shot blasting, and polishing processes. The entire production process is fully automated, ensuring high precision and minimal defects. Our annual production capacity reaches up to 45,000 tons of finished products.
