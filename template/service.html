PROFILE WITHOUT
PROCESSING (Mill Finish)

About service

Aluminum profile produced by hot extrusion without subsequent surface treatment. Ideal for companies planning their own painting, anodizing, or mechanical processing. Provides excellent strength and geometric precision. Manufactured from 6060 and 6063 alloy series, available in heat-treated conditions (T5, T6). Wide range of shapes: structural, window and door, façade, and special profiles.
